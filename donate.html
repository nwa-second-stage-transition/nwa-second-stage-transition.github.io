Donate


Any contributions are appreciated. Our main goal for monetary donations will go to help pay for a new residential home. We are planning on using this home to help residents break the bondage of unhealthily living by learning the skills necessary to move to independence, safe, healthy, and productive adults.

Volunteer

Volunteers are a key part of helping our clients. If you are can help in one of the following areas, we would be interested in talking with you..

  • Financial Planning
  • Transportation
  • Health & Wellness
  • Life Skills Training
  • Mentor
  • Art Therapy Classes

Current Needs

  • Female Clients: shampoo/conditioners, body wash, lotion towels, socks, etc.
