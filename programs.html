Programs

Phase 1 is a short-term emergency shelter (1-2 weeks) service for individuals who have completed a recent half-way residential housing, shelter, or treatment program, but are awaiting approval to get into an apartment.

Phase 2 seeks to provide a safe and supportive short-term residential living (six months) service to women who want to live self-sufficiently safer lives; make informed decisions and undergo personal growth.

Phase 3 confirms the residents readiness to move to indepentant living.

Residential Manifesto

  • To reduce the chain and bondage of substance abuse, sexual assault, dating violence, stalking, homelessness, denial, anger, bargaining, depression and unhealthy dating lifestyles through acceptance of life skills and other programs.
  • Will assist individuals or families to continuing the process through the collaboration with other halfway houses, education and community resources.
  • Acceptance into the NWA Second Stage Transition shall base on eligibility.
