Hey Everyone!

We are holding an online auction to raise money for Northwest Arkansas Second Stage Transition starting on Thu July 15th, 2021 @ 6:00 AM CDT and ending on Sun July 18th, 2021 @ 9:00 PM CDT.

Please forward this email to anyone you know that may be interested in helping!

Your generosity and assistance is what keeps Northwest Arkansas Second Stage Transition thriving. We hope that you will participate in the auction! Thank you for your continued support.
